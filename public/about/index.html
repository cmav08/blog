Domain Expansion: Greenland of ten thousand concepts

Congratulations reader😎, you have reached the domain of CMav.

In this blog, I nerd out about fields that interest me, ranging from electronics, robotics, AI models, football(soccer) and programming languages. More importantly, it is an outlet for me to share interesting, educational, informative, and technical articles that may help you, the reader, easily augment your daily lives with technology.

Some of the topics that you might come across are:

  • My experiences (obviously)
  • My opinions (again obviously)
  • Programming concepts
  • Programming Frameworks/Libraries
  • Electronic Circuits
  • Review of popular research papers
  • How-tos (guides on using various technologies)

Also you might be wondering the meaning of the first sentence. Well, a “domain expansion” is basically a technique that is featured in the anime Jujutsu Kaisen. It can be utilized by both jujutsu sorcerers and cursed spirits to extend the range of their abilities within the area of a constructed physical boundary. Thus, anyone who enters a domain will be at the mercy of the creator’s abilities without having to be in direct contact with the creator- you should get the point by now😂. But don’t fear, there will only be good and informative content in this domain. So you will definetly be leaving unscathed and probably even better off than before you visited.
